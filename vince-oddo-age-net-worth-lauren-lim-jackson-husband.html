    Vince Oddo Family

    Vince Oddo comes from a family of four.

    His father, Vincent J Oddo was born in 1954 in Cheektowaga, New York. He attended Cheektowaga Central and Erie CC.

    Likewise, Vince’s mother, Suzanne Oddo was born on Sep 10, 1955, in Cheektowaga, New York.

    Together, Vincent and Suzanne Oddo worked as supervisors at Occhino Corp.

    On Suzanne’s 60th birthday, Vincent took to his FB to write, “A very Happy Birthday to my “Rock”, Love her more today than when we first met  46 years ago. Happy Birthday.”

    As for his sibling, Vince’s brother Nicholas M Oddo was born in December 1988. He is a State University of New York College graduate.

    Sadly, Vince’s grandparents, Ignatius J. Oddo and Domenica Margaret L. have long passed away.

    Trivia: Vince’s parents-in-law are named Tunina L and Laurence E Jackson. They turned 72 and 73 years of age, respectively, in 2023.

    Vince Oddo Career

    Vince Oddo has a diverse background, excelling in various roles throughout his career. Currently (as of 2023), he serves as the General Manager of Mosaic Bar & Grille at Montage Hotels & Resorts in Laguna Beach, California. He oversees the bar, pools, and beach service, managing a team of 80 employees and ensuring excellent customer satisfaction.

    Alongside his hospitality career, Vince is an accomplished Voice Over Artist represented by Atlas Talent Agency. He has over 500 days of motion capture experience and is skilled in various dialects, lending his voice to commercials, video games, and more.

    Previously, Vince has held management positions in the restaurant industry, including being the General Manager of a restaurant in Laguna Niguel, where he managed staff, scheduling, inventory, and events. He also has extensive acting experience, having worked as an actor, director, and stunt director in various projects, including Broadway productions and television series.

    On top of that, he’s also acted in movies and TV shows such as The Wrong Path (2021), Strange Times (2020), and God Friended Me (2019).

    Vince’s wide-ranging skill set also includes heavy equipment operation and coaching services in acting and stage direction. With his strong leadership and entertainment background, Vince Oddo brings a unique and valuable perspective to every endeavor he takes on.
