    Introduction to Arte Moreno

    Arte Moreno, born Arturo Moreno, is an American businessman best known for being the first Mexican-American to own a major sports team in the United States. His journey to wealth and prominence is a classic tale of entrepreneurship and passion for sports. Moreno’s business acumen has been honed over decades, leading to his current status as a billionaire and a prominent figure in the sports industry.
