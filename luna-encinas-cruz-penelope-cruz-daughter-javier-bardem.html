Luna’s parents, Penélope and Javier, dated each other for a long time. The long-time couple exchanged their wedding vows back in July 2010  and have been together ever since.

    Luna is not the only child of the Oscar-winning actress Penélope. She has an elder brother named Leo Encinas Cruz. The absolutely adorable Leo was born in January of 2011 in Madrid, Spain, two years prior to Luna.

    Luna Encinas Cruz Is The Only Daughter Of Penélope Cruz And Javier Bardem

    Penelope and Javier were over the moon with the arrival of their new baby girl — Luna Encinas Cruz. She is the pair’s second child and their only daughter. Luna was born at the Ruber Internacional Hospital in Madrid, Spain.

    Being a celebrity kid, Luna’s parents celebrated her 4th birthday party with a trip to Disneyland. Luna and her brother Leo traveled there along with their parents. Furthermore, Penelope’s brother Eduardo Cruz and his girlfriend also joined them on the trip. The family was accompanied by their personal bodyguards and a Disneyland VIP tour guide.

    Despite their fame as the A-class actors, Penélope and Javier have always been highly protective of their private lives. In 2011, Penelope said that she wanted her kids to grow up in a secret way as possible.

    Penélope Doesn’t Want Her Daughter Luna To Get Any Media Limelight

    There is no doubt that Academy Award winner, Penélope is very media conscious for her daughter Luna. The actress doesn’t involve her in any media confrontation.

    Not only that but, Penélope doesn’t even have a TV in her house. She doesn’t want her children to grow up in front of the screen. The gorgeous actress said,

    “I worry for the future generations of children that spend hours every day on video games or television or they are given a phone at 11 or access to the internet and no control of the time of using it.

    They are not supposed to see the material they are not ready to digest yet but I don’t have anything against a few minutes of a cartoon that is age-appropriate,”

    It looks like one of the sensual actresses of Hollywood is undoubtedly a tough mum for her children. However, the decision seems very fruitful for the upbringing of the 9-year-old Luna.
