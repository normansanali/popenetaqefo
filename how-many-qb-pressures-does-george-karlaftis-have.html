    George Karlaftis, a defensive end who was born in Greece, competes for the Kansas City Chiefs of the National Football League. His birthday is April 3, 2001. He was trained as a multi-sport athlete, playing water polo for the Greek national team in addition to basketball, football, track and field, and other sports. He was a native of Athens.

    Karlaftis was born in Greece’s capital city of Athens. Growing up in Greece, his father Mathiós “Matthew” Karlaftis excelled in all sports. After graduating with a degree from Purdue University, he continued his education by studying civil engineering at the University of Miami.

Karlaftis declared for the NFL Draft in 2022 after posting 4.5 sacks in 2021.

    How many QB pressures does George Karlaftis have?

    According to Pro Football Focus, Karlaftis has 37 total pressures, 29 hurries, and four quarterback hits.
