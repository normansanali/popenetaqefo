    TEEN Mom Leah Messer’s ex-fiancé Jaylan Mobley has moved out of their $500k West Virginia home.

    The two announced earlier this week that they are going separate ways just two months after he proposed in Costa Rica.

    Jaylan, 26, was pictured on Thursday moving out of the huge five-bedroom pad that he bought for Leah and her three daughters in March.

    Photos showed the US Army Cyber Officer looking dejected as he shifted his belongings from the house to a U-Haul truck parked outside.

    According to TMZ, he is moving into a nearby apartment, while Leah, 30, and the girls will be staying put.

    However, their future in the home is uncertain, as The U.S. exclusively reported in May that Jaylan only put his name on the deed of the house.

    The luxurious home features plenty of space for Leah and her three daughters from her previous marriages;  Addie, nine, and twin girls Ali and Aleeah, 12.

    Leah has remained tight-lipped about the reasons behind her and Jaylan’s split.

    TMZ claims that the Teen Mom personality has told friends that she is saddened by the breakup, and that she thought her relationship with Jaylan would go the distance.

    The former couple announced their split via a shared statement to Instagram on Tuesday.

    As well as five bedrooms, the 6,000-square foot house also boasts a games room and a sauna.

    The Charleston property sits on .37 acres of land, and Jaylan even bought a dog to complete their new family in April.

    Cracks began to show between the pair during the final season of Teen Mom 2 as they realized they were in different places of their lives with regards to marriage and children.

    Unlike twice-divorced Leah, Jaylan has never been married before and has no kids of his own. 

    He is also four years her junior.
