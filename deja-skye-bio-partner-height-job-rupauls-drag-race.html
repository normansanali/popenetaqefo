    VH1’s RuPaul’s Drag Race season 14 premiered on the first Friday of 2022, but it was a two-part premiere! Yes, only 7 contestants were introduced in the first half, and the rest 7 joined in on the 3rd episode. The “do-it-all” queen, DeJa Skye was one of them who finally appeared on the show after two auditions.

    Get to know her more as this DeJa Skye Bio proceeds. 

    DeJa Skye On RuPaul’s Drag Race

    “We’re going to dominate this season,” DeJa assured her fans even before  RuPaul’s Drag Race season 14 started. As for “we” in her context, DeJa referred to her hometown Fresno, where she started her career a decade ago.

    This season, DeJa was joined by co-stars Alyssa Hunter, Angeria Paris VanMichaels, Bosco, Daya Betty, Jasmine Kennedie, Jorgeous, June Jambalaya, Kerri Colby, Kornbread “The Snack” Jeté, Lady Camden, Maddy Morphosis, Orion Story, and Willow Pill.

    Also, the drag race tradition was broken this season, after a straight, cisgender man, Maddy was cast on the show.

    All excited to appear on the show, DeJa took it to her IG on Dec 3, 2021, to write, “DREAMS REALLY DO COME TRUE Are you ready to Embrace the Curve? We’re making big moves when DragRace Season 14 premieres.”

    DeJa Skye Pronouns And Gender

    DeJa Skye defines herself as a “drag” who’s interested in men.

    As for her pronouns, DeJa is cool with however people address her. Also, since she often split her time in and out of drag, there’s no specific pronoun she likes.

    Does DeJa Skye Have A Partner?

    DeJa Skye didn’t seem to have a partner as of early 2022. Also, she put Facebook relationship status to be “single” then.

    However, many stars cherish privacy in their dating life. So, DeJa might have a secret girlfriend, for all we know. But publicly, she wasn’t dating anyone then.

    DeJa Skye Height

    DeJa Skye stands tall at a height above 5 feet 8 inches (173 cm).

    Mentioning her district features, DeJa has brown eyes, an oval face, and she often shaved her head to rock that wig.

    DeJa Skye Job

    DeJa Skye is a drag performer. She started her career over a decade ago and has worked in places including The Fifth Quarter, FAB Fresno, and Splash Fresno. But that was just her night job. 

    By day, DeJa worked as a dance and cheer choreographer. Her students (then in seventh and eighth grades), even won the Jamz Fresno regional competition in November 2020, earning a spot in the national competition in Las Vegas 2021.

    Sadly, DeJa stopped her choreographer job, after 13 years in October 2021. Saying goodbye to all her colleagues and students, DeJa wrote over FB, “It has been a rollercoaster of an experience, but I wouldn’t change a thing.”

    Also, she added that she was off to a “different endeavor” then.

    This self-described “WHOA-man”, DeJa is “colorful, cheeky, and curvaceous.” She designs her costumes, paints gorgeous mugs, and rocks the stage. So, what made her so appealing? Well, undoubtedly, it was her personality, and her confidence to be unapologetically herself.

    “She’s a big old nerd. That was something that helped Skye land her spot on the show this season. This time around, she was 100 percent herself,” said Clinton Moss, DeJa’s general manager at Splash.

    As to answer what got her into drag, DeJa recalled that it was season 1-2 of RuPaul’s Drag Race initially. “It intrigued me, then seeing the local queens I thought ‘if they could do it I could do it,’” she shared.

    DeJa defined her style to be “extreme” when it came to styling. She loved playing with colors that evoked emotions.

    Trivia: DeJa already has an IMDb profile, all thanks to her time in RuPaul’s Drag Race season 14.

    Has DeJa Skye Revealed Her Family?

    Yes, if you check her FB, she’s tagged a lot of family members over it.

    Listing them: Vicente Sustaita (Son), Madison Heart (sister), and Emma Fèmme Brûlée,  Melody De Leon, Krooz Joey and Rafael Alvarez (all are brothers).

    Also, DeJa has two drag daughters.

    DeJa Skye Without Makeup

    DeJa appears beautiful as ever even without makeup. Just imagine her without a wig, and add some shaved mustache and beard lines. That’s her real self.

    Also, she often wore hats when she wasn’t wearing a wig.
