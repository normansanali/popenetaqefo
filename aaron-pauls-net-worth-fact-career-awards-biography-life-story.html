    Aaron Paul Sturtevant, popularly known as Aaron Paul is an American actor. He is best known for his role as Jesse Pinkman in the AMC series Breaking Bad. His role in the series won him several accolades along with his co-star, Bryan Cranston.  He has also appeared in numerous films and television series. He has also done several voiceover roles in films and television series. He started his career as a model, later becoming a popular actor. 

    What is Aaron Paul Famous For?

    • His role as Jesse Pinkman in AMC series, Breaking Bad.

    Where was Aaron Paul Born?

    Aaron Paul was born on 27 August 1979. His birth name is Aaron Paul Sturtevant. His birth place is in Emmett, Idaho in the United States. He holds American nationality. He was born to a father, Robert Sturtevant, and a mother, Darla Sturtevant. He has 3 older siblings. His zodiac sign is Virgo. He has Scottish, German, and English ancestry and belongs to White ethnicity. 

    He attended Centennial High School in Boise, Idaho. He graduated from high school in 1997. After graduating from high school, he moved to Los Angeles in his Toyota Corolla with his mother and $6,000 savings. He also worked as a movie theater usher at Universal Studios in Hollywood.

    Modeling Career

    He competed and finished second in the 1996 International Modeling and Talent Association competition. 

    He then started appearing in the music videos and television commercials. 

    He appeared in music videos for the Korn song “Thoughtless”, “Screaming Infidelities”, and the Everlast song “White Trash Beautiful”. 

    Television Career

    He made his television debut in a minor role in the teen drama series, Beverly Hills, 90210 in 1999. 

    He appeared on an episode of television game show, The Price is Right, where he lost Dice Game and overbid by $132 on the Showcase. 

    He later guest-appeared in several television series before gaining fame. 

    He had guest appeared in Melrose Place, Suddenly Susan, 3rd Rock from the Sun, Get Real, 100 Deeds for Eddie McDowd, The Division, Nikki, The Guardian, The X-Files, Judging Amy, NYPD Blue, CSI: Crime Scene Investigation, Birds of Prey, Wasted, ER, Kingpin, CSI: Miami, Guiding Light, Threat Matrix, Life of Fire, Veronica Mars, Joan of Arcadia, Point Pleasant, Criminal Minds, Sleeper Cell, Bones, and Ghost Whisperer. 

    He landed one of the main roles as Scott Quittman in the television drama series Big Love in 2007. He rose to prominence after this role. 

    He gained worldwide recognition for his role as Jesse Pinkman in the AMC series, Breaking Bad in 2008. 

    His character Jesse Pinkman was originally meant to die during the first season. But the series creator Vince Gilligan modified the original scripts to keep the role until its final season. 

    He later landed the lead role as Eddie Lane in drama web television series, The Path in 2016. 

    He landed voiceover main role in animated series, BoJack Horseman in 2014. 

    He also has voice roles in Robot Chicken, Tron: Uprising, and Black Mirror.

    Film Career

    He made his film debut in the teen comedy film, Whatever It Takes in 2000.

    His major films are Need for Speed, Mission: Impossible III, Exodus: Gods and Kings, Decoding Annie Parker, Hellion, and Central Intelligence among others.

    His other minor films are K-PAX, National Lampoon’s Van Wilder, Perfect Opposites, Bad Girls from Valley High, Choking Man, Daydreamer, Say Goodnight, The Last House on the Left, Wreckage, Smashed, A Long Way Down, Eye in the Sky, Fathers and Daughters, Triple 9, The 9th Life of Louis Drax, Come and Find Me, American Woman, and Welcome Home.

    He has also appeared in short films, Candy Paint, Leo, Weird: the Al Yankovic Story, and Quirky Girl. 

    He has voice roles in films, Help! I’m a Fish and Kingsglaive: Final Fantasy XV.

    He will reprise his role as Jesse Pinkman in the film adaptation of popular series, Breaking Bad. The film is titled El Camino: A Breaking Bad Movie. He is also the producer of the film. The film is set to release in October 2019.

    Awards

    His role Jesse Pinkman in the popular series, Breaking Bad won him several accolades. They are

    2009, 2011, and 2013 Saturn Awards – Best Supporting Actor on Television for Breaking Bad.

    2010, 2012, and 2014 Primetime Emmy Awards – Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Drama Series for Breaking Bad.

    2011 PRISM Award – Male Performance in a Drama Series for Breaking Bad.

    2013 Satellite Award – Best Supporting Actor – Series, Miniseries, or Television Film for Breaking Bad.

    2013 Screen Actors Guild Award – Outstanding Performance by an Ensemble in a Drama Series for Breaking Bad. 

    2014 Critics’ Choice Television Award –  Best Supporting Actor in a Drama Series for Breaking Bad. 

    Who is Aaron Paul Married To?

    Aaron Paul is a married man. He is married to Lauren Parsekian. The couple met each other at the Coachella Festival. They got engaged on 1 January 2012. They later tied a knot to each other on 26 May 2013. Before his wedding, he emailed the song “Beauty” by The Shivers to everyone on the guest list and asked them to learn the words so they could sing along during the ceremony. The couple welcomed their first child, a daughter, Story Annabelle in February 2018.
