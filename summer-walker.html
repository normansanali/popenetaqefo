    Summer Walker (born April 11, 1996) is an American Singer, Songwriter, and Social Media Star from Atlanta, Georgia. She is best known for her first mixtape titled “Last Day of Summer” which came out on October 19, 2018.

    She worked with the collaboration of labels like LoveRenaissance and Interscope Records for her first commercial project. It peaked in the 44th position on the Billboard 200 chart.

    Furthermore, Last Day of Summer grabbed number 25 rank on the Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums as well. On October 4, 2019, Summer released her debut studio album “Over It”.

    The album contains singles such as “Playing Games” and “Stretch You Out”. The song titled Girls Need Love (Remix) featured Drake (Canadian rapper, singer, record producer, and businessman).

    Over It received the number second position on the US Billboard 200 List. Some of her other popular works are Clear (Extended Play), CPR, Girls Need Love, Stretch You Out, Playing Games, Drunk Dialing…LODT,  Fun Girl, Like It, Just Might, etc.
