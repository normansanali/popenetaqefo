    TikTok star and social media influencer who is best recognized for sharing lip synch videos and other comedy content through her account for her over 5 million fans. She is also known for sharing lifestyle clips and food reviews which have also contributed to her over 200 million total likes. 

    Before Fame

    She first started gaining attention by sharing photos through her Instagram in June of 2018. She then expanded to TikTok in August of 2019 and went viral quickly with her first food review earning over 800 thousand views and a ForYou page feature. 

    Trivia

    She partnered with Fashion Nova. She is also known for her YouTube channel where she shares additional vlogging and mukbang videos that have earned her over 600,000 total views. 

    Family Life

    She was born and raised in San Diego and is also known for her Vietnamese heritage. Her younger brother David has a popular TikTok account at davdngo.

    Associated With

    She shared a Duet with fellow TikTok star Mariano Castano through her account in December of 2019.
