    JB GOT7 Career

    Im Jae Bum formerly known as JB and now Jay B is a South Korean songwriter, singer and actor under the H1GHR Music group. He debuted in the year 2012 as half duo of the music duo JJ Project with Park Jinyoung under JYP Entertainment. He debuted as the leader of GOT7 in 2014.

    Together with Yugyeom, Jay B debuted as Just, a subunit of GOT7 with their hit EP Album titled Focus and the single Focus on Me.

    In the year 2021, after his contract with JYP Entertainment has ended, he and his groupmates left the agency to pursue solo and group activities. He went solo under his stage name Jay B. Despite him going solo, he was still leading GOT7.
