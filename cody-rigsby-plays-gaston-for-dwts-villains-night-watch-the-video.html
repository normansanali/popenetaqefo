    Cody Rigsby Plays Gaston For DWTS' Villains Night - Watch The Video!

    Cody Rigsby channels his inner Gaston for Villains Night on Dancing With the Stars on Tuesday (October 12). The 34-year-old Peloton instructor showed off his strength on the dance floor with pro partner, Cheryl Burke, dancing a Viennese Waltz to Gaston from Beauty and the Beast.

    12 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Cody Rigsby channels his inner Gaston for Villains Night on Dancing With the Stars on Tuesday (October 12).

    The 34-year-old Peloton instructor showed off his strength on the dance floor with pro partner, Cheryl Burke, dancing a Viennese Waltzto “Gaston” from Beauty and the Beast.

    The judges gave Cody and Cheryl some constructive criticism, after both were sidelined from breakthrough COVID-19 the week before.

    Bruno Tonioli told him that he’s “so happy to see you in a full production,” and while it “needs a bit of polish,” Cody knows how to command a stage.

    Len Goodman added that the performance was “fantastic”.

    Cody and Cheryl earned 31 points for the night. Bringing their total score, which was combined with Heroes Night, to 58 out of 80.
