    MrBeast established himself as one of the most prominent figures in the digital platform. He diversifies his content across his multiple YouTube channels and TikTok account as he is now counted among the top five most-followed personalities on that platform.

    As of 2023, his estimated net worth stands at $100 million, with a monthly income ranging from $3 to $5 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. This substantial wealth derives not only from his YouTube earnings but also from various lucrative business ventures, such as his merchandise sales, the popular Beast Burger chain, and the sweet delights of Feastables.

    After dating fellow YouTuber Maddy Spidell from June 2019 to 2022, MrBeast went out of his way to meet his now-girlfriend, Thea Booysen. Booysen made her debut appearance in one of MrBeast's YouTube videos, titled $1 Vs $100,000,000 Car!, which was uploaded in September 2023.

    How Much Does Thea Booysen Make From Her Multiple Careers?

    Thea Booysen is a digital streamer and e-sports caster from South Africa. She started streaming in December 2017 after she created a Twitch channel under the moniker TheaBeasty. However, success on the platform didn't come quickly. It took 30 months before she achieved Twitch Partner status and channel growth.

    Within her streaming content, Booysen has shared her gaming adventures with a variety of titles, including Gwent: The Witcher Card Game, Expedition Agartha, The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt, and more. Her channel boasts over 22.9 thousand followers and a few hundred subscribers. She also maintains a gaming YouTube channel where she shares highlights from her Twitch live streams, accumulating over 225 videos and amassing more than 18k subscribers with a total of 1.6 million video views.

    Booysen also holds the official title of esports caster for CD Projekt Red (CDPR) and is most frequently spotted casting for the game Gwent: The Witcher Card Game. Her casting experience extends to major events such as Gwent World Masters 1, Gwent World Masters 3, and Season 3: Open 1.

    Beyond her online career, Booysen has a strong educational background, holding a bachelor's degree in psychology. She furthered her studies at the University of Edinburgh, where she successfully attained a master's degree, specializing in neuropsychology.

    In August 2022, Booysen released her debut book, The Marked Children, which has received positive reviews on Amazon. She sells her book in paperback for $13.
