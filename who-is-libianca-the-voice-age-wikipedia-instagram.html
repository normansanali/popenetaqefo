    Who Is LiBianca from The Voice?

    LiBianca is one of the 48 artists of The Voice. Similar to her unique name, she also got a unique talent for singing.

    The name LiBianca comes with a story. Her parents were having a hard time deciding what to call her.

    LiBinaca’s dad wanted to call her Liane while her mom thought Bianca was a good choice. And hence they chose the middle ground.

    Bianca was added to the Li from Liane. She started singing when she was 7 years old and held her first guitar 3 years later.

    The newcomer on The Voice has already released two of her singles this year. She has also collaborated with many other singers, such as M3tro.

    The Thankyou singer dedicated her song to her parents and siblings. They helped her throughout her challenges and were always there to support her.

    LiBianca describes herself as an over-sensitive and over-emotional person. She also holds deep gratitude towards her African community and dedicates the song, Special Lovin.

    Find about her Age and Wikipedia Profile

    LiBianca has not revealed her age till now. And we also cannot find her on any of the Wikipedia profiles. But we got you covered on her.

    The Voice singer seems to be in her early 20s as we observe her appearance. She was born in St. Paul, Minnesota.

    But the American singer spent most of her childhood in her home country back in West Africa with her parents. It seems she started following her passion early on.

    LiBianca got her inspiration from her babysitter, who used to sing gospel for her. She was already writing songs and singing them in church when she was 10 years old.

    When LiBianca was 13 years old, she moved back to the US with her father. She continued the rest of her studies in the US.

    LiBianca is finally on Instagram

    LiBianca made her first Instagram post in January. Her fans can finally get a sneak peek into her personal life under the username @iamlibianca.

    The popular pop star has garnered 2,791 followers with only 14 posts till now. She recently shared her The Voice debut. Don’t forget to follow her.
