    Bryce Papenbrook Biography

    Bryce Papenbrook is an American Voice Actor who was born and brought up in Los Angeles, California, United States of America. He is widely known for his voice work for notable production companies including “Viz Media”, “Animaze”, “Bang Zoom! Entertainment”, “Funimation”, “Studiopolis” and “Scoffer Studios”. His voice acting roles includes ‘Danganronpa’ series as Makoto Naegi and Nagito Komaeda, ‘My Hero Academia’ as Yo Shindo, and ‘Attack on Titan series’ as Eren Jaeger.

    Bryce has voice acting roles in several series and animations such as ‘The Seven Deadly Sins’ as Meliodas, Zeldris, Demon King, ‘ Miraculous: Tales of Ladybug & Cat Noir’ as Adrien Agreste/Cat Noir, Félix, ‘ My Next Life as a Villainess: All Routes Lead to Doom!’ as Alan Stuart, and in ‘Ruff Ruff, Tweet, and Dave’ as Hatty the Hamster.

    Bryce Papenbrook Martial Art

    Papenbrook has trained in the martial arts for over 16 years and holds a fourth-degree black belt. He started training in the Chuck Norris System of Tang Soo Do under his original instructor Steve Clark. Later,  he would train and receive his fourth-degree black belt in the system under Clark’s instructor, former Chuck Norris black belt Dennis Ichikawa.

    Consequently, he would go on to become a 3-time kickboxing champion with over 60 fights to his credit. Besides, he has been a kickboxing instructor at the House of Champions Academy of Martial Arts in Van Nuys, California.
