    Early Life

    The Arizona Cardinals wide receiver was born as Larry Darnell Fitzgerald Jr on August 31, 1983, in Minneapolis, Minnesota, USA, under the star sign of Virgo. 

    Larry is the son of Larry Fitzgerald Sr and late. Carol Fitzgerald. His father Larry Sr is the Living Legend Legacy Award-winning TV personality and sportswriter who works for the Minnesota Spokesman-Recorder. 

    Fitzgerald Jr's mother Carol passed away in April 2003 due to a brain hemorrhage. The footballer Larry also has a brother named Marcus Fitzgerald.

    Concerning education, Larry graduated from the Academy of Holy Angeles, located in Minneapolis. Later, he attended the University of Pittsburgh.
