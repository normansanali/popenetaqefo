Answer The ABS light on your Kia Sorento illuminates when the vehicle fails a self-diagnostic cycle. When it is activated, it signifies that the Sorento does not have Anti-Lock brakes, and hence does not have the added safety that they give. The system will apply the greatest amount of braking to a wheel that is beginning to lock up in order to prevent the vehicle from sliding.
