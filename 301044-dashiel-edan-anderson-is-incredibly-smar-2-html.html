    Dashiel Edan Anderson, actress Milla Jovovich's daughter, has won millions of fans online. Dashiel is one of Milla's three children, which include Ever Gabo Anderson and Osian Lark Elliot Jovovich-Anderson.

    As Dashiel Edan Anderson grows, she becomes more like her famous mother, Milla Jovovich. Although the future is uncertain for Dashiel, she has shown glimpses of her mother's fun personality.

    Milla's two other daughters and Dashiel have sparked fans' curiosity. Not much is known about Milla's motherhood life, except that she is a highly talented person in show business.

    Milla Jovovich, her daughters Dashiel Edan Anderson and Ever Gabo Anderson and her husband Paul W.S. Anderson arrive at Charles-de-Gaulle airport on July 1, 2017 in Paris, France. | Source: Getty Images

    Born in Ukraine, Milla is a multi-talented Hollywood star who has graced over 100 magazine covers as a supermodel while also excelling as a fashion designer, singer, and renowned public figure.

    Her thrilling performances in films like "Ultraviolet," as well as her lead role in the "Resident Evil" series, have left an indelible mark on the film industry. Despite her success in show business, little is known about her life as a mother.

    Dashiel Edan Is Milla Jovovich's Second Child with Paul W.S. Anderson

    In August 2014, Milla announced on Facebook that she and her husband, Paul W.S. Anderson, were expecting a child. The couple decided to postpone performing movie stunts. Milla's post read:

    "Between the stunt work and what will become my ever-expanding belly, we didn't think pregnancy and zombie killing is the best combo! I imagine the only thing I'll be killing in the near future is an endless supply of cupcakes."

    Milla discovered she was pregnant just before her trip to South Africa to film "Resident Evil: The Final Chapter." Instead of continuing with the production, she announced that they would have to postpone it until after the baby's birth.

    Milla Jovovich and Paul W.S. Anderson walk with their two dogs on March 11, 2015 in Los Angeles, California. | Source: Getty Images

    The couple welcomed their daughter Dashiel in April of that year at 5:45 in the morning. The couple's representative announced that the family was thrilled with the new arrival and expressed their joy in a statement.

    Milla expressed her admiration for Dashiel and declared that her daughter was the best companion to spend time with.

    Shortly after giving birth, Milla sparked a baby fever among her followers after posting an adorable photo of Dashiel on her Instagram account. The baby's cute pout and stunning eyes were enough for Milla to describe her as a "smushy face."

    Milla Jovovich Has Been Documenting Dashiel's Growth on Her Instagram

    Over the years, Milla has been very proud to show off the cutest pictures of her daughter, which show them having fun together. As Dashiel grows up, she resembles her mother more daily.

    Milla shared several photos of her and her daughter with silly facial expressions, showing their fun side. She revealed that they could spend hours in bed, making each other laugh with silly faces.

    The proud mother recently shared photos of her and Dashiel bonding in their urban garden. Dashiel picked fresh vegetables with her grandfather and helped him water the plants.

    Milla shared some adorable pictures of Dash having a blast at her Easter-themed fourth birthday party in a beautiful garden filled with stunning floral decorations. The happy mom remarked that her daughter is an "incredibly smart and perceptive little person."

    Dashiel has become fearless and revealed a fun-loving personality. Milla shared a picture of Dashiel in Paris, where her colorful hair was on display.

    Additionally, Dashiel was fascinated with makeup, experimenting with it during Halloween 2022 with her mother. Milla shared that they put on "zombie makeup," and Dash reveled in her "spooky spirit."

    In December, Milla and Dashiel enjoyed the winter season. Milla captured their joyful moment by taking a selfie together. Milla was wrapped up in a cozy, dark winter coat, while Dash was dressed in a cheerful blue onesie with vibrant patterns.

    Milla expressed her admiration for Dashiel and declared that her daughter was the best companion to spend time with. "These are the moments that live forever in my memories," she shared in a separate post.

    Dashiel Edan Has Two Sisters, One of Them Is Already a Promising Model and Actress

    Dashiel is not the only child of Milla and her husband. Dashiel has two siblings—her big sister, Ever Gabo Anderson, born in 2007, and her youngest, Osian Lark Elliot Anderson, born in 2020.

    Ever is following in her mother's footsteps as she pursues a career in acting and modeling. She made her acting debut in 2017 when she played the younger version of her mother's character, Alice, in "Resident Evil: The Final Chapter."

    She has appeared as the young counterpart of Scarlett Johansson in the Marvel film "Black Widow" and is set to star as Wendy Darling in Disney+'s "Peter Pan & Wendy."

    Ever always knew that her fate was tied to the industry. As a child, she declared her desire to become an actress through her imaginative play with her dolls. Ever joins the ranks of children with celebrity parents, such as Zoe Kravitz, Maya Hawke, and Dakota Johnson.

    Ever has also made a name for herself in the fashion world, as she is the new face of the brand Miu Miu and has 800,000 followers on her TikTok account, which her parents monitor.

    Not much is known about Osian's future, but at the moment, she is happy being the cutest baby in the family. Milla's Instagram post mentioned she was like a little lark as she sang nursery rhymes all day, making "sounds of pure joy."
