    Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott Live Together

    Kylie Jenner wants you to know that she and her boyfriend Travis Scott are living under the same roof. Although the beauty mogul recently revealed that she's living with her best friend, Jordyn Woods, she recently clarified that she and Scott also have a place together.

    24 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Kylie Jenner wants you to know that she and her boyfriend Travis Scott are living under the same roof. Although the beauty mogul recently revealed that she's living with her best friend, Jordyn Woods, she recently clarified that she and Scott also have a place together.

    "I’m actually scrolling through Instagram right now, and it says that me and Travis don’t live together, and so I just want to say that we never miss a night with each other, we go back and forth from my Calabasas house and the city home that we actually got together," she said in a new interview, as reported by Us Weekly.

    Jenner, 21, and Scott, 26—who are parents to seven-month-old Stormi Webster—first struck up dating rumors at Coachella in April 2017, which the reality TV star says was their first date. Since then, they've kept their relationship fairly private and make sure to give each other space, they revealed in their GQ cover story last month.

    "[If] he has events or something, I won't come," Kylie said. "Because I want him to do his own thing. I want him to be him. I don't want it to be Kylie and Trav. If people don't ever see us together, that's okay with me, because we just do our thing."

    Kylie recently hinted on Instagram that she and Stormi will be joining Travis for his upcoming Astroworld tour, so you can expect to see the family of three together on the road soon.
