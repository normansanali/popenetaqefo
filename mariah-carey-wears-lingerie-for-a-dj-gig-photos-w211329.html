    What a vision! Mariah Carey went pantsless during a hosting gig on Sunday, June 26.

    The “Obsessed” singer had an after-hours gig at Las Vegas’ 1 Oak nightclub following a performance for her Caesars Palace residency. Instead of slipping on one of her glamorous gowns, the mom of twins Monroe and Moroccan, 5, showed up in just lingerie. Carey, 46, styled a black lace corset with thigh-high fishnets held up by a garter belt, and wore a pair of skimpy shorts that barely covered her derriere, paired with a strappy pair of Christian Louboutin pumps. To cover up, she slipped on a black leather jacket … but it didn’t stay on once she hit the booth to pick out tunes with DJ Suss One. And of course, she couldn’t leave home without some kind of sparkle. She picked a glittering diamond teardrop necklace, diamond hoops and her signature butterfly rings.

    Carey’s lingerie look wasn’t her only wild moment of the weekend. The fiancée of businessman James Packer pulled another hot mama onstage for a lap dance during her Saturday, June 25 show: Blac Chyna! Chyna, 28, sat on a bed while Carey’s male dancers blindfolded her and treated her to a striptease.

    “You’re so awesome, they don’t know what to do!” Carey told Chyna before tickling her with a pink feather.

    While onlookers may have been surprised at Carey’s revealing wardrobe choice on Sunday, she recently admitted that lingerie in broad daylight is the norm for her. In an April 18 Instagram, she posed in front of a pizza oven wearing a white negligee. “It was real! That’s what I walk around in!” she told PrideSource. “I barely own any clothes! All I have is friggin’ lingerie.”
