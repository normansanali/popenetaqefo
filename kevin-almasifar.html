    Kevin Almasifar Biography

    Kevin Almasifar was born in Tehran, Iran on May 18, 1996. Kevin’s parents lived in Tehran at the time of his birth, but 7 years later, his parents relocated to India, where he settled in Pune, Maharashtra. He obtained his primary and secondary studies at Pune.

    He attended Bharti Vidyapeeth School for his secondary education. Following that, he graduated from a college in Pune. Kevin stated in an interview that as a youngster, his classmates teased him for being a foreigner. When he was ridiculed by peers at school, he became interested in martial arts. From the time he was in elementary school, he was involved in sports such as martial arts. Because of his love of sports and ridiculing peers as foreigners, he disguised himself as a mixed martial arts fighter at an early age. After that, he began MMA training while still in India.
