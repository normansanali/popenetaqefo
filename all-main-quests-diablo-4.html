    Here is a list of all the main quests of Diablo 4.

    Diablo 4 is the latest RPG game by Blizzard Entertainment. The game is the newest edition of the famous Diablo RPG franchise. Diablo is famous for its unique quests and side quests. Players will go through different situations and face different enemies on their way to complete all the quests and finish the game.

    Diablo 4 has brought even better quests for the players, than its predecessors. There are six acts along with one prologue in the list of the main quest of Diablo 4. In this article, we will provide you with a list of all the main quests of Diablo 4. Diablo 4 features a lengthy list of side quests too alongside its main quests.

    Act 1 – A Cold and Iron Faith

    Act 2 – The Knife Twists Again

    Act 3 – The Making of Monsters

    Act 4 – A Gathering Storm

    Act 5 – Secrets Bartered, Fates Sold

    Act 6 – Dance of the Makers
