    Perth People Are Friendly

    West Australians are a warm, friendly, laid back bunch on the whole. They're used to being forgotten about by the rest of the country and, whilst they prefer to think WA stands for Way Ahead, they accept that Wait Awhile is equally applicable.

    Whats it like living in Perth?

    Perth has more of a suburban feel to it than other big cities because it is much more spaced out. As well as having your own safe suburbia to live in, you can also enjoy up to 320 days of sunshine a year, allowing you an active outdoors lifestyle and plenty of days at the beach.

    Is it worth living in Perth?

    Aside from its sharky waters and god-awful remoteness, Perth is an incredible place to live.     Is it always warm in Perth?

    In Perth, the summers are warm and dry; the winters are long, cool, and wet; and it is windy and mostly clear year round. Over the course of the year, the temperature typically varies from 48°F to 86°F and is rarely below 40°F or above 97°F.

    How hot does Perth get in summer?

    Summer in Perth is hot and dry with very little rainfall. Average temperatures range from 17.5 - 30°C (63.5 - 86°F). In the afternoons a sea breeze called the 'Fremantle Doctor' provides some relief from the heat.
