    Cassandra Church is an American actress, producer, music department. She is well known for her appearance in Real Stream(2012), Nightstalker(2009), and Absolute Evil – Final Exit(2009).

    From the age of 5, she began to learn the arts of dance and martial arts and started competing in local talent shows, flexing her vocals and lending her voice to musical theater. Also, she took acting classes at Arts Ed in London and cast in several Theater Royal productions in Bury St Edmunds.

    She graduated from RAF Lakenheath American High School and later attended the American Academy of Dramatic Arts in New York City. Currently, she is located in Los Angeles.

    Currently, Church is the lead singer in the band Sing Orpheus known for five songs on REFUSED’s 2015 album “FREEDOM.”
