    John Goodman is an iconic actor who was born on June 20, 1952 in St. Louis, MO. He is best known for his role as Dan Conner in the TV show Roseanne, for which he won a Golden Globe Award for Best Actor in 1993. He has also starred in many other films such as Monsters, Inc., The Big Lebowski, and O Brother, Where Art Thou?
