    Who exactly is Alina Morse?

    Alina Morse is a teen entrepreneur and student from the United States. She is the CEO and founder of Zolli Candy, a multimillion-dollar enterprise. In the United States, Zollipops is the best-selling sugar-free, healthy candy.

    In 2018, she was the youngest person to be on the cover of Entrepreneur Magazine.

    Alina Morse: Work, Life, and Profession

    Alina began dreaming about creating a lollipop that would not decay teeth while eating candies when she was seven years old. She began experimenting with candies in her home oven, stove, and microwave. The formula took two years to develop thanks to extensive online study, consultation with a food scientist, and input from her dentist on the ingredients.

    In 2014, she invested $3,750 while developing a formula and presented their first retail placement to Whole Foods Market. They sold 70,000 Zollipops in the first year, and sales more than doubled each year from 2014 through 2018. Kroger, America’s largest supermarket chain, chose Zollipops in 2016. Soon after, Zollipops were available for purchase on Amazon. Zollipops was Amazon’s best-selling sugar-free hard candy and lollipops in 2018.
