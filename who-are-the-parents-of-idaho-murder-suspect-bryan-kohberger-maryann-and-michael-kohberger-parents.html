    Instead of forming incorrect assumptions, Kohberger’s parents supported the “presumption of innocence” for their son.

    Arrested For Four Idaho Murders Is Bryan Kohberger

    Kohberger was given a public defender’s position and held without bail at the Monroe County Correctional Facility in Stroudsburg.

    Bryan and his father, Michael, were detained twice in Indiana, Kohberger’s public attorney, Jason LaBar, informed the reporters on January 2.
