    As noted, Kurt Angle’s ex-girlfriend, former TNA Knockout Trenesha Biggers, popularly known as Rhaka Khan, . Biggers, who is a strong advocate of exposing domestic violence, went to social media to reveal the tumultuous and abusive relationship between her and Angle during a very low time in his life.

    Apparently other websites have been falsely reporting Biggers was offered “hush money,” however this is inaccurate, according to Biggers herself. Discussing this matter with her on numerous occasions, Biggers stated that it was not her intention to be offered hush money, nor bribe Angle in any way. Instead, she genuinely wanted to make sure that Angle received help, so someone else would not go through what she did.

    This is what Trenesha expressed to me about the situation.

    “[Websites] are FALSELY reporting that I accepted “hush money.” That is 100% wrong. I was NEVER paid ANYTHING. I had a full time high paying salary job at a University while I was in TV, and about 50K in my savings. TNA was my [extra money].”
