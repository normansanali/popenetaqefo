    Rihanna is back with yet another New York City winter street style look, this time accompanied by boyfriend A$AP Rocky.

    The couple was photographed last night while departing Pastis, a Parisian bistro in the Meatpacking District. For the date, the two coordinated in their own versions of fur-trimmed outfits. The rapper wore an all-black outfit, including straight-leg trousers, boots, gloves, and a zip-up jacket with a black boa trim framing the hood.

    Meanwhile, Rihanna maintained her sporty streak in yet another pantsless look. Dressed in a blue-and-white zipper track jacket by Martine Rose with a furry striped Prada shawl nonchalantly thrown over one shoulder, the Fenty mogul capped the look off with a Saint Laurent miniskirt; the electric-blue boucle tweed piece featured a pine-green faux-fur trim on its A-line hem. For accessories, she opted for a few chain necklaces and a pair of white pumps with straps crisscrossing up to right below her knees.

    Getty Images

    Getty Images

    The "Needed Me" singer has been on a wintry street style run lately, with many of her looks going sans pants. Earlier this month, she was spotted on two separate occasions wearing an oversized jersey as a dress, styling one with a bright-red parka and the other with a vintage Jean Paul Gaultier coat.

    Rihanna has also previously worn tracksuits by Martine Rose, the British-Jamaican menswear designer. In December, she was spotted in New York City styling an oversized black tracksuit from the label with a quilted Miu Miu coat.
