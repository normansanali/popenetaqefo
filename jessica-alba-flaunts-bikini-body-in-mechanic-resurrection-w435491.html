    Fine-tuned! Jessica Alba revealed her rock-hard bikini body in new stills from her upcoming movie, Mechanic: Resurrection, out this coming Friday, August 26.

    In a scene from the action film, the Honest Company cofounder, 35, lies out on a white sandy beach in a baby-blue bikini with tiny straps. She similarly amped up the sex appeal at promotional events for the flick, in which she stars alongside Jason Statham and Tommy Lee Jones. For a Friday, August 19, press conference in Beverly Hills, she stunned in a deep orange Victoria Beckham midi-length dress partnered with black leather Brian Atwood platform sandals.  

    Alba, who’s the mom of daughters Haven, 5, and Honor, 8, with husband Cash Warren, has famously worn teeny-tiny bikinis off camera, too. On July 19, she strolled on the sands of Hawaii in a white Tory Burch Mira top and matching string bottoms, both printed with pineapples. And on July 15, she looked regal in a simple purple string two-piece. Although her figure is enviable, she’s revealed that her fitness regimen is fairly simple.

    “I like to mix it up with dance class, spinning and hot yoga sculpt. Listening to loud pop and hip hop music and working out with a friend keeps my energy up,” she told Get the Gloss. “No matter what I do, it’s always important to work my core muscles — it’s not just for abs, it’s also for balance and posture.”

    And inner beauty is just as important to Alba as her outward appearance. “I believe that when you feel good on the inside it shows on the outside. I try to take time out to relax,” the mogul continued. “When I get the chance, I love to run a warm bath with some lovely aromatic candles and take the time to clear my mind and enjoy the peace — it helps me feel refreshed.”
