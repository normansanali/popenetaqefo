    Zoe Saldana is an English-American actress and model. Zoe Saldana is Famous for her blockbusters such as ‘Star Trek’, ‘Avatar’ and ‘Guardians of the Galaxy.’

    Early Life

    American actress Zoe Saldana was born on June 19, 1978, in Passaic, New Jersey, the U.S. She is 45 years old with American nationality. She has two siblings, Cisely and Mariel. When she was 9 years old her father died in a car accident.

    Then, she moved with her family to the Dominican Republic which was her father’s home country. Her biological father’s name is Aridio Saldana and her mother’s name is Asalia Nazario. After the death of her father, her mother remarried Dagoberto Galan.

    Career

    After her exposure in an episode of Law and Order (titled “Refuge, part 2”) in 1999, she became a member of Faces. Her first film was ‘Center Stage (2000)’ directed by Nicholas Hytner. Then, she appeared in a series of teen flicks, ‘Get Over It (2001)’ and ‘the Britney Spears vehicle Crossroads (2002)’.

    In 2003, she played a lead role as a female pirate in the surprise hit movie ‘Pirates of the Caribbean: The Curse of the Black Pearl’. Similarly, in 2004, she played the role of an immigration agent in ‘Steven Spielberg’s The Terminal’.

    In 2005, Saldana was seen in Constellation, Guess Who with Ashton Kutcher, and Dirty Deeds. She appeared in the romantic comedy dramas Premium (2006)’ and ‘After Sex (2007)’. In 2008, she played a supporting role as Angie Jones in the action thriller movie Vantage Point. She played two iconic roles in 2009.

    She appeared as Lieut and Uhura in the movie ‘Star Trek’, which redefined the characters of the original TV series, and through the technique of motion capture, she personifies Neytiri –a high-ranking member of the Navi, a humanoid race indigenous to the exoplanetary moon Pandora in James Cameron’s sci-fi film Avatar.

    More about her career

    In 2010, she starred in the main cast of ‘The Losers as Aisha al-Fadhil’, a native Bolivian woman. In 2012, she played a major role in the romantic drama ‘The Words’. Similarly, in 2014, she portrayed the character Gamora in the movie ‘Guardians of the Galaxy ‘. In the same year, she also performed in Rosemary’s Baby, a television adaptation of Ira Levin’s horror novel.

    In 2017, she returned as Gamora in Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2 released in May 2017. She played a major role in other blockbuster movies such as Avengers: Infinity War (2018) and Avengers: Endgame released in April 2019. In 2021, she starred in two roles made by Netflix, appearing as Rosita in the film musical Vivo and the titular character in Maya and the Three.

    Award and Achievements

    In 2009, she won the Best Ensemble Cast award for the movie Star Trek (2009). Likewise, in 2010, she won the Best Actress Award for Avatar (2009). In 2012, she won the Favorite Movie Actress Award from Colombiana (2011).

    In 2014, she won the Best Ensemble Award for Guardians of the Galaxy (2014). Then in the following year, she won the Best Actress award in The Book of Life (2014).

    In 2017, she won Choice Movie Actress Award for Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 2 (2017). Then in the following year, she won Best Supporting Actress in I Kill Giants (2017). In 2019, she was nominated for Best Cast Award from Avengers: Endgame (2019).

    Personal Life

    Talking about her personal life, she is currently married and has 3 sons. In June 2010, she engaged to her longtime boyfriend Keith Britton who is an actor and the CEO of My Fashion Database. In November 2011, they announced their break up after 11 years.

    She also had a relationship with the actor Bradley Cooper for two years. In March 2013, she started to date Italian artist Macro Perego Saldana. They married in June 2013 in London. Perego and Saldana have 3 sons, twins born in November 2014 and a third born in February 2017.
