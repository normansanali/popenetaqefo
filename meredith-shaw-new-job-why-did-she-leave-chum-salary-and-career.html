She is also a model, body acceptance campaigner, style expert, and singer-songwriter.

    Growing up in Toronto, her parents were both lawyers, with her mother serving as Dean of Osgoode Hall Law School. Her scholastic career includes studies in opera and theatrical arts at Queen’s University in Kingston and Branksome Hall in Toronto. Meredith announced her departure from CHUM, the radio station where she was a presenter, four days ago. People are interested in Meredith Shaw’s new employment and income information as a result of her disclosure. Let’s have a look at it.

    Meredith Shaw’s Next Move After Leaving CHUM

    Meredith Shaw said farewell to CHUM on August 18, 2023. As word of her departure from CHUM circulated, speculation about where she would go grew. Meredith has not revealed any details about her upcoming relocation. Interestingly, Chum1045 is still included on her Twitter profile. Her fans are anxiously awaiting further information from her about her forthcoming ventures. Meredith made the switch from Boom 97.3 to CHUM 104.5, an iHeartRadio station, in November 2014. On weekdays, she could be heard presenting the afternoon drive program from 3 to 6 p.m., and on Sundays, she could be heard hosting The Back in the Day Brunch from 11 a.m. to 1 p.m.

    Meredith formally announced her leaving after nine years of service there. The radio station also expressed their best wishes to her on this occasion, expressing their thanks and devotion with a statement that says,

    “Thank you from the bottom of my heart!” Until we meet again, please accept my love and thanks.”

    CHUM-FM is a 104.5 MHz Canadian radio station situated in Toronto, Ontario. The station, which is owned by Bell Media, broadcasts a hot adult contemporary genre. CHUM-FM’s studios are located at 250 Richmond Street West in the Entertainment District, and its transmission tower is atop the CN Tower.

    Meredith Shaw, as previously stated, has not disclosed any information regarding her new job since leaving CHUM. While her ardent fans are saddened, they are also excited about her next enterprise, which might lead to exciting opportunities. Her pay details, which remain unknown, are in a similar scenario. Her net worth is reported to be between $1 and $5 million USD. Meredith began her career as a singer-songwriter with the release of her first album “Place Called Happy” in 2011.

    That same year, she began working as a weekend presenter for Boom 97.3. This work gave her the opportunity to interview performers ranging from Serena Ryder and The Spoons to Gordie Johnson and INXS.
