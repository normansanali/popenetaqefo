    Rick Kiefer Obituary, Death Cause – Waterloo, Iowa and the world at large mourn the loss of a remarkable man, Ricky L. “Rick” Kiefer, who passed away on October 12, 2023, at the Cedar Valley Hospice Home in Waterloo. Rick’s life was a testament to hard work, dedication, and the relentless pursuit of knowledge. As we gather to remember and honor his life, we celebrate a man who made significant contributions to his community and the world, leaving a lasting legacy that will continue to inspire us.

    Rick was born on July 6, 1958, in Waterloo, Iowa, to proud parents Robert “Earl” and Mary “LaVerna” Butler Kiefer. He grew up in a close-knit family that instilled in him the values of integrity, hard work, and a thirst for knowledge. His formative years in Waterloo were the beginning of a journey that would see him reach incredible heights in the fields of agriculture and international diplomacy.

    A bright student, Rick graduated from Waterloo West High School in 1976, marking the end of one chapter and the beginning of an exciting journey into higher education. He pursued his academic passions at Iowa State University, where he honed his knowledge and skills. His academic journey didn’t stop there; Rick’s commitment to education led him to the American Institute of Business, where he continued to expand his horizons.

    Undeterred by the challenges that lay ahead, Rick embarked on a remarkable academic pursuit that would see him achieve the pinnacle of his educational dreams. He earned his PhD in Biology from the prestigious Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT). Rick’s journey from Waterloo to MIT was a testament to his unwavering determination, passion for learning, and dedication to making a difference in the world. But life had more in store for Rick than just academic accolades. On April 26, 1980, he married the love of his life, Julie A. Cool, at the First Congregational Church UCC in Waterloo. Their union marked the beginning of a partnership founded on love, mutual respect, and shared dreams. Together, they would create a life filled with love, laughter, and purpose.
