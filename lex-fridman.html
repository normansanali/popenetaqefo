    Family & Ethnicity

    His father, Alexander A. Fridman, is a plasma physicist. He has a brother named Gregory Fridman, who is the CEO of a company called AAPlasma LLC.

    Career

    In 2014, he joined Google as a researcher. He worked there for a year. In 2015, he joined the Massachusetts Institute of Technology as a researcher in human-centered AI, autonomous vehicles, and deep learning. In 2006, he opened his YouTube channel. He uploaded his first podcast ‘Ido Portal: Movement’ in 2014. His videos are based on research topics in artificial intelligence, deep learning, and autonomous vehicles. He has more than 465K people subscribed to his channel.
