    Who is Sneako?

    American YouTuber and social media star Nicolas ‘Nico’ Kenn De Balinthazy was born in New York City, USA, on 8 September 1998, making Virgo his zodiac sign. He’s known better on the internet under his nickname ‘Sneako’, which was the name of his two no longer active YouTube channels; Sneako was popular on the video sharing service mostly for his controversies including connection to British-American social media star Andrew Tate and his brother Tristan, and was banned on both channels on 3 October 2022 ,because of his constant violation of the network’s community guidelines.

    SNEAKO have 676K subscribers,Sneako is best known as a Content creator who went viral with the video “Would You Say the N Word for One Dollar?” He posts vlogs,internet rants and political videos as well.

    Sneako’s today active on the video sharing service Rumble, and has amassed over 250,000 followers.

    Education and early life

    Sneako was raised in New Haven, Connecticut, as his family moved there not long after he was born; he hasn’t shared many details about his parents, however, it’s believed that Sneako’s father was a mailman, and his mother a professional dancer. Because Sneako hasn’t disclosed whether or not he has siblings, he’s widely believed to be an only child.

    He studied at a local high school, and was interested in several activities during his time there, including acting in school plays, playing football and running track. He matriculated in 2016, and then chose to focus on pursuing a career on the internet, rather than to enroll at college.

    Sneako’s parents are deeply religious Catholics, and he went to church with them at least once a week while growing up.

    Most popular YouTube videos

    Sneako’s fan-made YouTube channel today holds some of his most popular videos, which had helped him amass over two million subscribers on his now-banned channels. Most of these featured him speaking to the Tate brothers, as he was one of their supporters, while he also spoke in Andrew’s defense after he was arrested in Romania. We’re going to write about the three most popular videos on the network, which are still contributing to Sneako’s popularity on the internet.

    His #1 video “SNEAKO’S PODCAST WITH ANDREW TATE!!! (EMERGENCY MEETING 5) FULL RUMBLE STREAM” was uploaded on 30 September 2022, and has since been watched over 100,000 times; it features Sneako making a guest appearance on Andrew Tate’s podcast “Emergency Meeting”.

    His second most popular video “SNEAKO REACTS TO FIRST TATE SPEECH ON RUMBLE” was uploaded on 27 August 2022 and has since been watched close to 70,000 times; it features Sneako watching and discussing Tate’s long speech on Rumble.

    Sneako’s #3 video “SNEAKO REACTS TO CORYKENSHIN” was uploaded on 25 August 2022 and has since been watched over 64,000 times; it features him reacting to American YouTuber CORYKENSHIN’s videos.

    Two more YouTube channels have been launched for the uploading of Sneako’s deleted videos: “Sneako Library” on 3 August 2022 which today numbers over foul million views of all its 147 videos combined, and is subscribed to by nearly 4,500 people. The second channel “Sneako Reuploads” was launched on 3 October 2022, and today numbers over 5,000 subscribers, while 41 videos have been posted onto it.

    Presence on social media networks

    Sneako’s built himself a fanbase on all of today’s popular social media networks.

    There are nearly 700,000 people following him on Instagram today, though he’s posted only 10 times; his pictures show him during his everyday life, whether he’s interviewing Andrew Tate, travelling around the world, or meeting former US President Donald Trump.

    Sneako joined Twitter in May 2012, and has tweeted nearly 20,000 times; he’s followed by close to 530,000 people on this network – most of his tweets are about politics and religion

    Nearly 2,000 people are following Sneako on Facebook, and his most recent update was on 14 November 2022.

    He’s steadily building himself a fanbase on TikTok, as he’s still quite new to this social media network; Sneako’s followed by more than 800 people, and both his videos combined have amassed nearly 100,000 likes; both videos feature Sneako showing strange animals.

    Controversies

    Sneako was perhaps mostly popular on YouTube for the controversies surrounding him, especially for his points of view which resemble those of Andrew Tate; we’re going to cover some of his ‘most popular’ controversies.

    He posted a video onto his YouTube channel on 15 September 2020, in which he stated that the French movie “Cuties” was one of his favorites; the film had previously been criticized for sexualizing children.

    After American YouTuber D’Angelo Wallace uploaded a video about Sneako, claiming that he belonged to the streets because he had cheated on his girlfriend, Sneako responded with mocking D’Angelo and referring to him as ‘a snitch’, ‘gay’ and ‘the sort of child who sat with the teachers at lunch’.

    Many people heavily criticize Sneako for his collaboration with the Tate brothers.

    Sneako joined the Ye24 campaign with Nick Fuentes and Kanye West in November 2022, and was criticized because Nick had previously made anti-Semitic and racist statements.

    Because Andrew Tate has recently converted to Islam, Sneako’s now planning to do the same.

    Love life and girlfriend

    Sneako’s been in several relationships, but only the name of one of his former girlfriends has been disclosed.

    He was in a relationship with American model Lily Fofana, who’s today followed by more than 13,000 people on Instagram; they broke up because Sneako had cheated on her.

    Because of his failed relationships, Sneako chose to have an open relationship with his new girlfriend in 2019; he then revealed that they attended swinger parties together, swapping partners, and that the experience left him traumatized. Sneako was then ridiculed on the internet, with people calling him a ‘cuckold’ and ‘cuck’.

    He seems to be unattached as of June 2023, hasn’t married and doesn’t have children.

    Height, eyes and wealth

    Sneako’s age is 24. His hair and eyes are brown, he’s 6ft 2ins (1.88m) tall and weighs around 180lbs (82kgs).

    Sneako’s net worth’s been estimated at over $1 million, as of June 2023.
