    Wedding bells are in the air for Julia Stiles and Preston J. Cook! The actress is officially off the market, and she proudly showed off her brand new bling on Instagram, where she posted a gorgeous photo of her unique sparkler -- and we have all the details surrounding her engagement ring! It's bound to be a holiday she'll never forget! Julia Stiles, 34, shared a picture, hand-in-hand with her main man, Preston J. Cook, with a pink heart emoji and the caption: "Best Christmas Ever" -- and all we could focus on was the pretty sparkler on that finger! Yup, Julia debuted her new engagement ring! The star took to Instagram on Jan. 4 to show off her pretty new present, a stunning rose gold creation, with the gorgeous water at Isla Grande, Las Islas De Rosario behind her.

    So, what do we know about Julia’s brand new bling? Expert celeb jeweler, Shari Fabrikant of Robert Fabrikant Inc. in NYC, filled us in on the gorgeous creation. “Looks like an approximate 2.0ct rose cut diamond set in an 18kt rose gold setting. Ring is very modern and simple. This style completely focuses on the stone. It has very clean look and is very minimalistic. Very refreshing for her to sport such a low key ring,” she said. “If the stone is a F color VS2, the ring would run app $35,000.”

    It sounds like we’re seeing the start of a new trend — and Mark Keeney, VP of Marketing at Ritani, agreed! “Julia appears to have a simple yet elegant bezel set round diamond within an increasingly popular rose gold setting.”

    Rose gold jewelry is having a major moment, so it comes as no surprise to see the trend surface during the engagement season — and we’re loving how modern and elegant her setting is!
