    Buck Sexton is an American radio host, political analyst, author, and former intelligence officer with the Central Intelligence Agency.

    He is the host of Premiere Networks’ The Buck Sexton Show, a three-hour weekday discussion radio show that airs on over 100 stations across the United States.

    Sexton started his career as an officer in the Central Intelligence Agency.

    After working for a year in Counter-Terrorism Centre, he was transferred to Iraq and then Afganistan Office.

    Moreover, after working for four years with the CIA, he joined the New York Police Department Intelligence Division.

    In 2019, he also became the national security editor of The Blaze. He then started as a host in talk shows.

    Buck Sexton’s Bio

    Buck Sexton was born on December 28, 1981, and grew up in Manhattan’s Upper East Side.

    In New York City, he attended St. David’s School for elementary school and Regis High School, a private Jesuit university-preparatory school.

    Sexton went to Amherst College in Amherst, Massachusetts, after finishing primary school. He received his Bachelor of Arts in Political Science with honors in 2004.
