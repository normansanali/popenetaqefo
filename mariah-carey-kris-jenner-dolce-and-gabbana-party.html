    Mariah Carey and Kris Jenner Wear Contrasting Looks at Dolce &amp; Gabbana Party

    Mariah Carey and Kris Jenner made a coordinated fashion statement while attending a recent Dolce &amp; Gabbana party. On Instagram, Hollywood's resident momager shared photos of herself posing alongside the Grammy-winning singer on a beach at night.

    04 Sep 2024, 00:00

    Mariah Carey and Kris Jenner made a coordinated fashion statement while attending a recent Dolce & Gabbana party.

    On Instagram, Hollywood's resident momager shared photos of herself posing alongside the Grammy-winning singer on a beach at night.

    In the pictures, Jenner wears a showstopping lacy white gown with organza ruffle trim on the collar, down the center, and on the sleeves.

    Meanwhile, Carey cuts a stylish figure in a body-hugging gown that seems to be a sultry take on pajama wear. Wearing the collared, pinstriped dress unbuttoned for a deep-V cut, she aptly shows off her D&G bralette and accessorizes with plenty of diamonds and even a tiara.

    Jenner captioned the post, "Me and Mariah… Go back like babies with pacifiers @MariahCarey @DolceGabbana."

    Jenner made a string of fashionable appearances during Paris Fashion Week earlier this month. In one memorable outing, Jenner accompanied her granddaughter North West to Balenciaga's couture show, where mom Kim Kardashian showed off creative director Demna's latest offerings by walking the runway.

    The entertainment mogul also recently opened up about her and her family's new Hulu show, The Kardashians, in an interview with BAZAAR.com.

    Of her grandkids growing up around cameras, Jenner said, "I think they're a little young. They're all so involved in all their activities and their lives and their schools. They're in the show from time to time, but they've kind of grown up on camera. I don't think it's anything special to them. It's just like, 'Oh, the cameras are here a little bit and this is what we're doing.' And sometimes they don't notice them at all, because we really do make such an effort to have them growing up so typical and doing lots of sports and activities. So they make the best of it."
